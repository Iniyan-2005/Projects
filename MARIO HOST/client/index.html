It is me mario!!!! hi!!!

🍄 My Origins — 1981

Back in the day, I was just a humble carpenter named Jumpman, running around dodging barrels to rescue Pauline from a big, angry ape named Donkey Kong. That was my first time in the arcades. People didn’t know me yet... but they would.

🔨 1983 – Meet My Bro!

Then came Mario Bros, where I switched careers — from carpenter to plumber. Why? Because the game was set in the sewers of Brooklyn! I teamed up with my younger brother, Luigi, and together we started squashing turtles and cleaning pipes. Family business, you know?

🌈1985 – Super Time!

Boom!I hit the big time with Super Mario Bros on the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES). It was me, the Mushroom Kingdom, Princess Peach, Goombas, Koopas, and of course — that fire-breathing turtle tyrant, Bowser. It was side-scrolling magic, and it changed video games forever.

✨The Power-Ups!

🍄 Super Mushroom – Made me grow big! 🌟 Star – Invincible Mario, baby! 🔥 Fire Flower – Pew-pew fireball Mario! Each one added a new twist to my adventures.

🌎Worlds & Galaxies

I didn't stop there! I went 3D in Super Mario 64, surfed through space in Super Mario Galaxy, and even became a cat in Super Mario 3D World. I've driven go-karts, played tennis, golf, soccer, and even had epic Smash Bros. battles!

👨‍👦Family & Friends

you know Luigi. But there’s also: Yoshi – my dino buddy 🦖 Toad – the loyal mushroom pal 🍄 Princess Peach – always getting kidnapped... but always staying strong 👑 Wario & Waluigi – ugh… the weird cousins 😅

🧢 2017 – I Got a Hat With Eyes!

Super Mario Odyssey took me around the globe — and my hat Cappylet me possess Goombas, tanks, and even a dinosaur! Yeah. I’ve really leveled up.

🧱 Today...

I’m still jumping, still saving the day, and still shouting "Let’s-a go!" Whether it’s the Switch, mobile phones, LEGO sets, or the Super Mario Movie, I’m still around — making people smile, one 1-Up at a time. So yeah... that’s my journey from a pixel plumber to a global icon. Wanna go on an adventure with me next? 🍄🚀
